Delicious Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a cake pan. In a bowl, mix sugar and cocoa powder. Add flour, eggs, and milk. Stir until well combined. Pour into the prepared pan and bake for 30 minutes.
