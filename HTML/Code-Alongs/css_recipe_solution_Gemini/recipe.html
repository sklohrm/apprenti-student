Delicious Chocolate Cake

The ultimate recipe for rich, moist, and unforgettable chocolate goodness.

Prep time: 20 min | Cook time: 30 min

Ingredients

  • ✓ 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ✓ 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ✓ 1 cup all-purpose flour (sifted)
  • ✓ 2 large eggs
  • ✓ 1/2 cup whole milk (room temperature)

Instructions

  1. Prepare: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Generously grease and lightly flour a 9-inch round cake pan.
  2. Combine Dry: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and cocoa powder until thoroughly combined.
  3. Add Wet: Add the eggs and milk to the dry ingredients. Stir using a whisk or electric mixer on low speed until the batter is just smooth.
  4. Bake: Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pan. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Cool & Serve: Cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then invert onto a wire rack to cool completely. Frost and serve!
