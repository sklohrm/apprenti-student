Cheesy Bacon Ramen

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of chicken ramen
  • ¼ stick of butter
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • ¼ cup of cheddar cheese
  • Frank’s RedHot Sauce, to taste

Steps

  1. Put water on to boil for ramen.
  2. While water boils cook bacon to desired doneness.
    Break into pieves when done and set aside.
  3. Follow ramen instructions for cooking on stovetop.
    Cook until al dente.
    Drain broth, leaving around 2 tbsp in the pot.
  4. Add cheese, butter, and RedHot Sauce to ramen, mix until all elements are fully incorporated.
  5. Transfer contents to bowl, top with bacon, and enjoy!
